Esteemed Launches New Website and Expands Service Offerings with Personal and Business Divisions

Aayush Mittal
February 03, 2025

Esteemed unveils new Personal Services membership program and announces strategic growth plans with expanded business offerings and acquisitions.

Olympia, WA – February 3, 2025 – Esteemed, a leading Staffing and HR Tech company, today announced the launch of its newly redesigned website alongside a significant expansion of its service offerings. Esteemed has introduced distinct Personal and Business divisions, marking a major evolution in the company’s mission to empower individuals and businesses to thrive in the modern workplace.

Esteemed has built its reputation through its comprehensive talent solutions, which combine advanced technology products like its Applicant Tracking System (ATS) with a community-driven Colleagues platform, a talent and opportunity marketplace boasting 20,000+ members. Beyond staffing services, Esteemed has supported its community through professional development events and sponsorships. However, with changing workplace dynamics, it became evident that providers and professionals within the Esteemed network needed greater access to tools and support to sustain long-term success.

New Personal Services Division – Empowering Individual Growth

To address this need, Esteemed has launched a Personal Services line in the form of a Paid Membership Program, offering members access to group purchasing power and discounts on critical services. Similar to platforms like Founders Card, the Esteemed membership program includes exclusive discounts on health insurance, hospitalization, pet insurance, legal services, and other voluntary benefits. Along with priority access to 4,000 co-working locations, virtual office options like business communications, and address — coupled with mail forwarding.

Members also gain access to tailored career advice and coaching, including guidance on transitioning from freelance work to business ownership. Additionally, Esteemed is introducing Colleagues Premium, which prioritizes members' access to career growth tools and personalized support across their journey. Full details on membership offerings can be found on the Esteemed website at Personal Membership Pricing and Small Business Pricing. The program will continue to add benefits and evolve over time.
 

 

Enhanced Business Services – Specialized Support for Enterprises and Small Businesses

Esteemed’s Business Division now offers targeted services designed to support both small businesses and enterprises. These services include specialized staffing solutions, Human Capital Management (HCM), and Business Enablement Software, all supported by Esteemed’s expert network of Colleagues. New services include:

  • HR Advisory, People Strategy, and Interim HR Leadership
  • Employer of Record
  • Managed IT & Digital Services

These expanded services are further strengthened by Esteemed’s ongoing acquisition strategy, targeting both successful staffing firms and select software companies. Through these acquisitions, Esteemed aims to provide clients with an unparalleled blend of people and technology-driven solutions for career and the modern workplace.

A People + Software Model Driving Innovation

Esteemed’s approach integrates the expertise of its Colleagues members with state-of-the-art technology, including HR tools such as its ATS, talent analytics, and collaboration platforms. This combination allows clients to benefit from high-touch, customized support tailored to their needs. The company’s growth plans reflect this commitment, as Esteemed seeks to become a dominant force in staffing, managed services, and HR technology.

“Esteemed has always been committed to enabling and empowering our community of professionals,” said Chris McGrath, CEO of Esteemed. “This expansion into both Personal and Business Services allows us to support individuals at every step of their career journey while offering businesses scalable solutions for talent and operations. We believe that by investing in both people and technology, we can transform the way companies and professionals collaborate, grow, and succeed.”

With a redesigned website and expanded offerings, Esteemed is poised to redefine what it means to be a modern workplace solution provider.

For more information, please visit www.esteemed.io.

 

About Esteemed:
Founded in 2019, Esteemed is a tech-enabled staffing and HR technology company driven by a network of 20,000+ professionals through its Colleagues platform. Esteemed offers a comprehensive range of personal and business products and services designed to empower modern professionals and organizations. The company specializes in career enablement, talent sourcing, human capital management, and managed services, underpinned by its innovative approach to building community, skill development, and delivering value to business customers through a flexible workforce and continuous innovations.
