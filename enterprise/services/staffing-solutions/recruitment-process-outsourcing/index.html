Questions we hear often...

What is Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO)?

Many people hear “Recruitment Process Outsourcing” and think of a staffing firm or headhunters. But with Esteemed, Recruitment Process Outsourcing is when you partner with experts to design and execute a customized talent acquisition and recruitment process that delivers exceptional business results. We work as one with your internal team to transform your approach to get the talent you need when you need it.

How Can Esteemed RPO Improve my Hiring Process?

Esteemed’s Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) streamlines hiring by combining AI-driven technology, industry expertise, and scalable solutions to reduce costs and improve hiring efficiency. Our flexible approach helps you fill positions faster, attract high-quality candidates, and enhance the overall hiring experience.

With Esteemed RPO, you gain:

  1. Smarter Talent Sourcing – Delivering the right message to the right candidates at the right time.
  2. AI-Powered Insights – Using data-driven technology to optimize hiring decisions.
  3. Scalability & Flexibility – Adjusting recruitment efforts to match your evolving needs.
  4. Skills-Based Hiring – Helping you build a future-ready workforce.
  5. Expert Guidance – Access to market intelligence and best practices for continuous improvement.

By outsourcing recruitment to Esteemed, your team can focus on business growth while we handle talent acquisition with precision and efficiency.

What is the difference between RPO and a staffing agency?

Staffing agencies focus on filling immediate hiring needs, such as sourcing candidates for temporary, contract, or seasonal roles. They provide quick fixes but don’t address long-term talent acquisition strategy or employer branding.

Esteemed RPO, on the other hand, is a comprehensive talent acquisition solution. We embed recruitment experts into your organization, aligning with your company’s values and workforce goals. Our AI-driven technology enhances hiring efficiency while maintaining a human touch, ensuring high-quality candidates at the speed and scale you need—all while improving the candidate experience and employer brand.

What is an RPO business model?

An RPO (Recruitment Process Outsourcing) business model provides organizations with outsourced talent acquisition solutions tailored to their specific hiring needs. At Esteemed, we offer four flexible RPO models:

  • Enterprise RPO – A full-service solution covering sourcing, interviewing, hiring, and onboarding.
  • Hybrid RPO – Supports a specific location or business unit, combining your processes with our recruiters.
  • Project RPO – Designed for a set number of hires within a short timeframe.
  • High Volume RPO – Scales rapidly to meet large hiring demands efficiently and cost-effectively.

Each model ensures a strategic, data-driven approach to talent acquisition, helping businesses secure top talent while optimizing efficiency and cost.

What should you look for in an RPO provider?

When choosing a Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) provider, consider these three key factors:

  • Comprehensive Talent Solutions – Look for a partner that takes an end-to-end approach to talent acquisition, using talent science, technology, and data-driven processes to solve recruitment challenges.
  • Industry Expertise – The right RPO provider should have experience in talent acquisition, talent management, digital recruiting technology, and organizational consulting to drive hiring success.
  • Strategic Partnership – Choose a company that acts as a true extension of your team, understanding your people, workflows, culture, market, and industry to deliver tailored hiring solutions.

A strong RPO partner will not only fill positions but also enhance your entire talent acquisition strategy for long-term success.
